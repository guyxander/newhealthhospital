New Health Hospital

Taking The Lead

New Health Hospital have pioneered advanced medical care in Nigeria. From being the first private hospital in the country to successfully perform open-heart surgery to becoming the first hospital in sub-Saharan Africa to earn accreditation from the Joint Commission International (JCI). We are continually improving to ensure you receive the best treatment possible.

New Health Hospital is a 165-bed purpose-built multispecialty, tertiary care private hospital in Nigeria. We offer care across a range of specialty medical and surgical services. This is a first of its kind facility in Nigeria and will deliver high quality, and accessible healthcare to communities in Lagos and Nigeria at large.
